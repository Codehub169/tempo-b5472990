Our Eye Care Services

A complete spectrum of services to cater to all your vision needs, delivered with expertise and care.

LASIK & Refractive Surgery

Achieve clear vision without glasses or contact lenses. We offer the latest in bladeless, customized laser vision correction for a safer and more precise outcome, helping you see the world with new clarity.

  • Custom Wavefront LASIK
  • Contoura Vision & SMILE Procedures
  • Implantable Collamer Lenses (ICL)
  • Comprehensive Pre-operative Assessment

Advanced Cataract Solutions

Regain vibrant, clear vision with our advanced cataract surgery. We use micro-incision phacoemulsification and offer a full range of premium intraocular lenses (IOLs) tailored to your lifestyle and visual needs.

  • Robotic Femtosecond Laser-Assisted Cataract Surgery (FLACS)
  • Monofocal, Toric & Multifocal Premium IOLs
  • Quick, Safe, and Painless Procedure
  • Personalized Lens Selection Counseling

Glaucoma Diagnosis & Management

Early detection and expert management of glaucoma are key to preserving your sight. We provide comprehensive diagnostic services and advanced treatment options, from medication to minimally invasive surgery.

  • Tonometry & Perimetry (Visual Field Testing)
  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) for Nerve Fiber Analysis
  • Medical, Laser (SLT, LPI), and Surgical Treatments
  • Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery (MIGS)

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Specialized care for the youngest eyes. Our friendly environment and expert team ensure your child's vision develops healthily, addressing conditions like squint, lazy eye, and childhood refractive errors.

  • Squint (Strabismus) Evaluation and Surgery
  • Amblyopia (Lazy Eye) Therapy and Management
  • Childhood Refractive Error Correction
  • Congenital Eye Disorder Screening

Retina and Vitreous Services

Comprehensive care for conditions affecting the retina and vitreous, including diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and retinal detachments, using state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment modalities.

  • Fundus Fluorescein Angiography (FFA) & OCT Angiography
  • Intravitreal Injections (Anti-VEGF)
  • Laser Photocoagulation for Retinal Disorders
  • Vitreoretinal Surgery for Complex Cases
